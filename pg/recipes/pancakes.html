Fluffy Homemade Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 large egg (or 1 individual applesauce cup = ½ cup applesauce)
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp oil of choice
  • 1 cup gf all-purpose flour with xanthan gum
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a medium bowl.
  2. Spray griddle or skillet with cooking spray and heat on low heat (between 3 and 4 on my stove works well).
  3. Scoop batter with ¼ cup measuring cup and cook about 2-3 minutes until pancakes start to bubble and puff.
  4. Flip pancakes and cook for another 2 minutes until golden brown.
