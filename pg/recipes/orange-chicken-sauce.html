Orange Chicken Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • ¼ tsp chili flakes
  • ½ tsp ginger
  • 1 Tbsp garlic
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce or coconut aminos
  • 2 Tbsp water + 2 Tbsp cornstarch

Instructions:

Mix and boil. Serve with fried rice and cubed chicken.
